Daily Reference

Do every day
  • Saline nasal rinse — morning + pre-workout
  • 10 min slow warm-up before every session
  • Breathe through nose during jogging
  • 120–140 g protein daily
  • Stay 1,750–1,900 kcal/day
  • 3.0–3.5 L water daily
  • 7–8 hours sleep
  • Vegetables at every meal
  • Rhinitis medication consistently
  • Pray and connect with family
Always avoid
  • Maida, white bread, biscuits, rusk
  • Packaged juices, sodas, energy drinks
  • Deep-fried snacks
  • Excess sugar in tea/coffee
  • Eating after 9 PM
  • Alcohol
  • Skipping warm-up
  • Sleeping under 6 hours
  • Skipping classes without reason
  • Judging progress by scale only
Calorie guide
  • Maintenance: ~2,100–2,250 kcal
  • Your target: 1,750–1,900 kcal
  • Deficit: 300–400 kcal/day (slow metabolism)
  • Fat loss: 1g fat = 9 kcal; 1 kg = ~7,700 kcal
  • 5 kg fat = 38,500 kcal total deficit
  • Timeline: ~4–5 months at this deficit
Protein sources
  • Eggs — 3–4 whole (don't skip yolk)
  • Chicken breast — 150–200 g
  • Soya chunks — 50 g dry = ~25 g
  • Paneer — 100 g = ~18 g
  • Dal / legumes — moong, masoor, chana
  • Greek curd — 200 g = ~15–20 g
Phase guide
  • Phase 1 (Wk 1–4): 4×/week, circuit + walk
  • Phase 2 (Wk 5–10): 5×/week, push/pull/legs
  • Phase 3 (Wk 11+): 5–6×/week, running
  • Fasted morning walk from Phase 3
  • Add only 2 reps/exercise/week
  • Track waist + chest monthly
Rhinitis management
  • Avoid outdoor training at dawn/dusk
  • Train indoors in weeks 1–3
  • No running before rhinitis is controlled
  • Elevate head of bed 15–20 cm
  • Fluticasone/Mometasone nasal spray
  • Stop if breathless beyond deconditioning
