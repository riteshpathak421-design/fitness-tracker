Daily Reference Guide

Do Every Day
  • 10 min slow warm-up before every session
  • 120–140 g protein daily
  • Stay 1,750–1,900 kcal/day
  • 8,000–10,000 steps daily
  • 7–8 hours sleep
  • Vegetables at every meal
  • Diaphragmatic breathing 10 min
  • Sunscreen SPF 30+ every morning
  • Pray and connect with family
Always Avoid
  • Maida, white bread, biscuits, rusk
  • Packaged juices, sodas, energy drinks
  • Deep-fried snacks
  • Excess sugar in tea/coffee
  • Eating after 9 PM
  • Alcohol — suppresses fat oxidation
  • Skipping warm-up before exercise
  • Sleeping under 6 hours
  • Skipping protein at any main meal
Protein Sources
  • Eggs — 3–4 whole eggs daily
  • Chicken breast — 150–200 g per meal
  • Soya chunks — 50 g dry = ~25 g
  • Paneer — 100 g = ~18 g
  • Dal / legumes — moong, masoor, chana
  • Greek curd — 200 g = ~15–20 g
Calorie Guide
  • Maintenance: ~2,100–2,250 kcal
  • Your target: 1,750–1,900 kcal/day
  • Deficit: 300–400 kcal (slow metabolism)
  • 5 kg fat = 38,500 kcal total deficit
  • Timeline: ~4–5 months at this deficit
  • Fat loss pace: ~0.3–0.5 kg/week
Phase Guide
  • Phase 1 (Wk 1–4): 4×/wk, circuit + walk
  • Phase 2 (Wk 5–10): 5×/wk, push/pull/legs
  • Phase 3 (Wk 11+): 5–6×/wk, running
  • Add only 2 reps per exercise per week
  • Fasted morning walk from Phase 3
  • Measure waist + chest every 15 days
Milestones
  • Week 2: habit forming, endurance up
  • Week 4: ~1 kg fat loss
  • Week 6: visible abdominal change
  • Week 10: 2–3 kg fat lost
  • Week 16: 5 kg fat, muscle gained
  • Day 15/30/42: measure waist + chest
