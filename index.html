📌 Daily Reference — Things to Remember & Avoid
DO EVERY DAY
  • Saline nasal rinse — morning + pre-workout
  • 10 min slow warm-up before every session
  • Breathe through nose during jogging
  • 120–140 g protein daily minimum
  • 3.0–3.5 L water daily
  • 7–8 hours sleep — non-negotiable
  • Vegetables at every main meal
  • Rhinitis medication consistently
  • 10 min diaphragmatic breathing
  • Weigh yourself fasted every morning
ALWAYS AVOID
  • Maida, white bread, biscuits, rusk
  • Packaged juices, sodas, energy drinks
  • Deep-fried snacks — samosa, pakoda, chips
  • Excess sugar in tea/coffee (>2 tsp/cup)
  • Eating after 9 PM
  • Alcohol — directly suppresses fat oxidation
  • Skipping warm-up before exercise
  • Skipping protein at any main meal
  • Sleeping less than 6 hours
  • Judging progress by scale alone
PROTEIN SOURCES
  • Eggs — 3–4 whole (don't skip yolk)
  • Chicken breast — 150–200 g per serving
  • Soya chunks — 50 g dry = ~25 g protein
  • Paneer — 100 g = ~18 g (watch cal)
  • Dal / Legumes — moong, masoor, chana
  • Greek curd — 200 g = ~15–20 g (pre-sleep)
RHINITIS MANAGEMENT
  • Avoid outdoor training at dawn/dusk
  • Train indoors in Weeks 1–3
  • Don't run before rhinitis is controlled
  • Head of bed elevated 15–20 cm
  • Discuss Fluticasone/Mometasone nasal spray
  • Discuss Montelukast with physician
  • Stop if breathless beyond deconditioning type
PHASE GUIDE
  • Phase 1 (Wk 1–4): 4×/week, circuit, walk cardio
  • Phase 2 (Wk 5–10): 5×/week, push/pull/legs split
  • Phase 3 (Wk 11+): 5–6×/week, running 2–5 km
  • Add only 2 reps per exercise per week
  • Fasted morning walk from Phase 3 onward
  • Track waist + chest, not just weight
MILESTONES TO WATCH
  • Week 2: Rhinitis stabilising, habit forming
  • Week 4: ~1 kg fat loss, endurance up
  • Week 6: Visible abdominal change begins
  • Week 10: 2–3 kg fat lost, clear strength gains
  • Week 16: 5 kg fat, muscle gained, 5 km run
  • Monthly: measure waist, push-ups, plank, HR
